The aim of the project was to:

"Investigate issues regarding the integration of the conservation of the Yunnan Golden Monkey with local development in Baima Xueshan Nature Reserve."

This was achieved through ecological research aiming to:

  • Study the ranging and habitat use of the monkey to establish its protection within the reserve
  • Gather population demographics to assess the threatened status of the monkey

And social research aiming to:

  • Assess local land use, firewood collection and hunting
  • Study attitudes to local conservation issues
  • Evaluate possible conflicts between park management and local interests
